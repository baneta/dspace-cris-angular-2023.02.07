By granting a license to the material you post, you define how others can use your work and under what conditions. Licenses enable individuals and institutions to grant the public permission to use their work in a standardized manner.

You can choose a license that allows anyone to freely use your material for any purpose, or opt for a more restrictive license that limits use to non-commercial purposes and/or prohibits the creation of derivative works.

When submitting to the openscience.ge digital repository, you can select from Creative Commons (CC) license, which offers several types, each with different permissions and restrictions.

If you're self-archiving already published materials, make sure to review the terms of the original publisher's license and adhere to those guidelines.

License Types

CC BY

Creative Commons Attribution v4.0 International

This license allows others to distribute, remix, adapt, and build upon your work, including for commercial purposes, as long as they give proper credit to the original author.

CC BY

CC BY SA

Creative Commons Attribution-Share Alike v4.0 International

This license allows others to distribute, remix, adapt, and build upon your work, including for commercial purposes, provided they give credit to the original author and license their adaptations under the same terms.

CC BY SA

CC BY ND

Creative Commons Attribution No Derivatives v4.0 International

This license allows others to distribute your work for both commercial and non-commercial purposes, provided that the work is not modified and the original author is properly credited.

CC BY ND

CC BY NC

Creative Commons Attribution Non-Commercial v4.0 International

This license allows others to distribute and adapt your work for non-commercial purposes only, as long as they attribute the original author. Adaptations are not required to be licensed under the same terms.

CC BY NC

CC BY NC SA

Creative Commons Attribution Non-Commercial Share-Alike v4.0 International

This license allows others to distribute and adapt your work for non-commercial purposes, as long as they provide attribution to the original author. Any adaptations must be licensed under the same terms.

CC BY NC SA

CC BY NC ND

Creative Commons Attribution Non-Commercial No Derivatives v4.0 International

This license allows others to distribute your work, provided they give proper attribution and do not modify it or use it for commercial purposes. This is the most restrictive type of Creative Commons license.
