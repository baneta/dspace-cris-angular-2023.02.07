openscience.ge is an open-access academic digital repository that aggregates the research outputs of academic and research-oriented organizations and individuals in Georgia. The repository is managed by the National Science Library of Georgia and its primary functions are to collect, store long-term, and provide access to scientific information.

Integrated into both the OpenAIRE network and the European Open Science Cloud (EOSC), openscience.ge became the first Georgian digital repository to join EOSC in 2022, supported by the National Initiatives for Open Science in Europe (NI4OS Europe) project. Further development followed in 2024-2025 through funding from the DataCite Global Access Fund (GAF).

The repository enables higher education institutions, research institutes, non-governmental organizations, state agencies, affiliated and independent researchers, as well as master's and doctoral students in Georgia, to create individual profiles and upload their research results. These can include scientific articles, books, conference proceedings, presentations, audio and video recordings, theses, and dissertations. A full list of accepted material types can be found in the User Guide.

Digital repositories and Their Benefits

Open-access repositories are digital platforms that collect, preserve, and provide long-term access to scientific information. They serve as digital archives for various types of research outputs, ensuring their accessibility and sustainability.

Benefits of Depositing Research in a Repository:

  • Open access and visibility
  • Increased impact of research results
  • Increased citations
  • Increased accessibility
  • Long-term storage of research results in a secure and standardized format
  • Stability of research results
  • Possibility of integration with other services (ORCID, DOI)

Become a Repository Contributor

To learn how to contribute to the repository, visit the User Guide, which provides comprehensive instructions on the submission process. The guide outlines who is eligible to contribute, the types and formats of materials that can be submitted, and how to properly prepare research outputs before submission. It also includes step-by-step instructions for uploading materials, available both in written form and through a Video Guide, ensuring a smooth and accessible submission experience.
